## 1️⃣ My Assignments Section

**What It Shows:**
Items where **you** are assigned as:
- Global Tech Lead, OR
- Global Business Lead, OR
- Market Success Owner

**Columns:**
- Request ID
- Request (title)
- Status
- Market
- Value (€)
- Action (Edit button)

**Use Cases:**
- See what's on your plate
- Quickly access items you own
- Track your workload

**Example:**
If your name is "John Doe" and you're the Global Tech Lead on REQ-0245, it will appear here.

---

## 2️⃣ Watching Section ⭐ NEW!

**What It Shows:**
Items where **your name** appears in the "Watchers" field.

**How to Watch an Item:**
1. Open any request (click Edit)
2. Scroll to "Watchers" field (in Portfolio Team section)
3. Add your name: `John Doe` (or comma-separated if others: `John Doe, Jane Smith`)
4. Save

**Columns:**
- Request ID
- Request (title)
- Status
- Owner (who's responsible)
- Market
- Last Update (submission date)
- Action (View button)

**Use Cases:**
- Follow items you're interested in (but not assigned to)
- Keep track of strategic initiatives
- Monitor items from your team/market
- Get visibility on cross-functional work

**Example:**
You're not the owner of "Sustainability Dashboard" but want to stay informed. Add yourself to Watchers field → it appears in this section.

---

## 3️⃣ My Submissions Section

**What It Shows:**
Items where **you** are the Requester (submitted by you).

**Columns:**
- Request ID
- Request (title)
- Status
- Owner (who it's assigned to)
- Market
- Submission Date
- Action (Edit button)

**Use Cases:**
- Track requests you submitted
- See who they're assigned to
- Check status of your ideas
- Follow-up on pending requests

**Example:**
You submitted REQ-0267 "Virtual Store Experience". It appears here regardless of status or who owns it now.

## 📊 Statistics Cards

**1. Total Requests**
- Total number of requests in the system
- All statuses, all users

**2. My Assignments**
- Count of items in "My Assignments" section
- Your current workload

**3. In Progress**
- Items with status "In Progress" or "Committed"
- System-wide metric

**4. Total Value**
- Sum of all Ballpark Values
- Displayed in millions (€M)

---

## 🎯 How the Sections Differ

| | My Assignments | Watching | My Submissions |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Filter** | You are owner/lead | Your name in Watchers | You are Requester |
| **Purpose** | Your work | Your interests | Your ideas |
| **Action** | Edit (you own it) | View (just watching) | Edit (your submission) |
| **Shows** | What you're responsible for | What you're following | What you initiated |

## 📝 Using the Watchers Field

### How to Add Watchers

**Option 1: When Creating a Request**
1. Click "+ New Request"
2. Fill out form
3. In "Watchers" field (Portfolio Team section), enter names:
   - Single: `John Doe`
   - Multiple: `John Doe, Jane Smith, Bob Johnson`
4. Save

**Option 2: When Editing a Request**
1. Click "Edit" on any request
2. Scroll to "Watchers" field
3. Add/remove names (comma-separated)
4. Save

### Who Should Watch?

**Good reasons to add someone as watcher:**
- ✅ Stakeholder who needs visibility
- ✅ Team member interested in outcome
- ✅ Person who can provide input
- ✅ Cross-functional partner
- ✅ Executive sponsor

**Not necessary to add:**
- ❌ Owner/leads (they're already assigned)
- ❌ Everyone (adds noise)

### Best Practices

**For Requestors:**
- Add relevant stakeholders when submitting
- Add your manager if they want visibility
- Add cross-functional partners

**For Portfolio Managers:**
- Watch strategic initiatives
- Watch high-value items (>€1M)
- Watch items with dependencies

**For Tech Leads:**
- Watch items using your tech stack
- Watch items that might block your work
- Watch items from related product areas

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

**Tip 1: Use Watching for Strategic Visibility**
Add yourself as watcher on high-priority items even if not assigned. Gives you visibility without workload.

**Tip 2: Track Your Ideas**
"My Submissions" shows impact of your contributions. Use to demonstrate value in reviews.

**Tip 3: Delegate But Monitor**
If you delegate work, add yourself as watcher. You're not the owner anymore, but stay informed.

**Tip 4: Cross-Functional Collaboration**
Add partners from other teams as watchers. Keeps everyone aligned without constant status meetings.

**Tip 5: Executive Dashboard**
Leaders: Set yourself as watcher on all strategic items. Your dashboard becomes your executive brief.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: How do I add multiple watchers?**  
A: Comma-separated in the Watchers field: `John Doe, Jane Smith, Bob Johnson`

**Q: Can I remove myself from watching?**  
A: Yes, edit the request and remove your name from Watchers field.

**Q: Do watchers get notified?**  
A: In POC: No (demo only). In real system: Yes (via Power Automate).

**Q: What's the difference between owner and watcher?**  
A: Owner = responsible for delivery. Watcher = interested party, no responsibility.

**Q: Can I watch items from other markets?**  
A: Yes! Add yourself as watcher on any request regardless of market.

**Q: Is there a limit on watchers per request?**  
A: No limit, but keep it relevant (typically 3-5 people).

**Q: Can I see who else is watching?**  
A: Yes, open the request and look at the Watchers field.

**Q: Why don't I see anything in My Assignments?**  
A: You're not assigned as owner/lead on any items yet. Check "Watching" or "My Submissions" instead.
